Module Reflections

These reflections are for ASU students only, and so if you are not an ASU student, you will not be able to access them; don't worry though, you're really not missing out on too much: we're just asking ASU students a few questions and encouraging them to reflect on the module. As a reminder, these are graded and due shortly after the end of the module.

You should not complete a reflection until you are "done" with the module (e.g., your next task is the next module; it's okay if you later come back to complete some late solves). The idea is to reflect back on the module, not just quickly complete the reflection for points. If you complete reflections excessively early, your reflection points may be reduced or removed.

We will make announcements in the course announcements channel, and update here, when reflections are released.
