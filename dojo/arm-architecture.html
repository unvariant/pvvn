ARM Architecture.

ARM processors are a competitor to the intel processors most of the dojo focuses on. Dominating the mobile computing space, these chips excel at power-efficient processing. Recently, ARM processors can also be found in personal computers, with Apple's M1 and M2 chips serving as notable examples.

This is an open-source community dojo exploring the aarch64 architecture. Pull requests are welcome! https://github.com/pwncollege/arm-dojo
