Reverse Engineering

Uncover the secrets hidden in code, binaries, or data. Analyze, deconstruct, and outsmart the protections to reveal what's really going on behind the scenes.

Is binary code Greek to you? There's learning material! This module will get you started; feel free to tackle it outside of the CTF!
